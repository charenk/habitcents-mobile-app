Floating toast times out before slow readers finish, steals focus, and reads as a system alert, not the product's voice. Rejected per spec ("not a toast, not a modal").

Why in-slot

Occupying layout (not floating) means the card never covers the primary action and never expires mid-read. It is terminal and calm: no close button, no "next", disappears on next visit.

Change answer

Same slot, #B2DFB6 30% tint, bold headline above. Identity beat. No modal, no confetti.

Why chapters, not confetti

  • The v2 arc counts total skips and never moves backward, so crossings are earned, not gamified
  • Identity copy ("someone who decides where money goes") is the strongest behavioral lever we have
  • Reuses the exact slot, so there is one coaching surface, not two
